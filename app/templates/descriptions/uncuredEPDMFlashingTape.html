Uncured EPDM Flashing Tape

RoofWrap Uncured EPDM Flashing Tape is an uncured EPDM flashing membrane laminated to cured butyl rubber tape and rolled onto a release liner.

Basic Uses

RoofWrap Uncured EPDM Flashing Tape was specifically developed for use in conjunction with RoofWrap Tape Primer to flash inside and outside corners, pipes or other penetrations that require the sealing flashing to be molded or conform to an irregular shape. RoofWrap Uncured EPDM Flashing Tapes are not permitted for use in stripping seams or batten bars.
