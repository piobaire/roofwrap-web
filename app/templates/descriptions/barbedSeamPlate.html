2" Barbed Seam Plates

RoofWrap 2" Barbed Seam Plates are 2 inch round Galvalume metal plates with reinforcing ribs and securement barbs.

Basic Uses

RoofWrap Barbed Seam Plates are used screws specifically for the mechanical attachment of RoofWrap RMS Tape.

Typical Physical Properties

RoofWrap plates are constructed from 26 gauge Galvalume metal.

Coating: Galvalume AZ 50 minimum
Corrosion: Meets FM corrosion standard 4470
Pull Thru: # 10 truss head pulled through the center hole of the plate, average pull thru: 577 lbs.
