RMS Tape

RoofWrap RMS Tape couples a 6"- 45 mil polyester scrim reinforced EPDM with a factory laminated 3"- 36 mil of cured EPDM splice tape laminated on one side.

Basic Uses

RoofWrap RMS Tape is attached either vertically or horizontally with RoofWrap Termination Bars or 2" Barbed Seam Plates and fasteners to the existing roof for securement below the RoofWrap EPDM field membrane at roof slope and angle changes. Use RoofWrap Tape Primer to prepare all surfaces to receive RMS Tape.
