Plumbing Vent Flashing Cap

Roofwrap Plumbing Vent Flashing Caps are a galvanized flashing cover that feature removable caps for cleaning access. They are installed with Roofwrap Caulk Tape and screws.

Basic Uses

Roofwrap Plumbing Caps are intended for use to fit over and seal around small diameter (2 1/2" ID or less) plumbing exhaust pipes.

Specifications

Roofwrap Plumbing Caps are made from galvanized steel. The cap assembly measures 3" tall and 5.5" wide at the base.
