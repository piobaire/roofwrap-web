Product Descriptions and Specifications

Drill Point Fasteners

RoofWrap Drill Point Fasteners are manufactured from C-1022 Cold Heading Wire with a low profile #10 truss with a hex head drive.

Basic Uses

RoofWrap Drill Point Fasteners are designed specifically for the mechanical attachment of RoofWrap Prefabricated Vent Hoods and Flashings, Hybrid Roof Attachment Strips and RoofWrap Termination Bar for edge termination and roof profile angle changes. Refer to the RoofWrap Installation Guide for installation instructions.
