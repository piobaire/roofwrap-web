RoofWrap Sealant

RoofWrap Sealant is a one-part, black or white, elastomeric sealant and adhesive designed for sealing the exposed/cut edges of field fabricated rubber membrane splices/laps, adhering roof mounted flashings and general purpose sealing needs.

Basic Uses

RoofWrap Sealant is required for sealing EPDM seams and flashings against the effects of weathering. Refer to the RoofWrap Installation Guide for appropriate use of Roofwrap Sealant when seams and flashings are completed with RoofWrap Tape Products.

Product Benefits

  • Excellent resistance to heat and moisture
  • Can be applied to wet surfaces
  • Good resistance to ozone and UV light
  • Adheres well to clean EPDM, metal, wood, glass, insulation and concrete
  • Easily extruded throughout application temperature range

Application Instructions

Remove all dust or other foreign material surfaces to be bonded. Clean the membrane thoroughly before application of RoofWrap Sealant. Bring sealant to room temperature prior to use at temperatures below 50F. Do not apply at temperatures below 30F. Apply a 1/4" to 3/8" bead of sealant along the edge to be sealed.
