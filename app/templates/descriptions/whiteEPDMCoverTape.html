White EPDM Cover Tape

RoofWrap White Cover Tape is an extruded, white rubber adhesive tape designed to provide high strength, watertight seals on white, single ply EPDM roofing membrane. This product is ideal for sealing over field installed Termination Bars. The product is soft and tacky for quick grab to EPDM and possesses high initial contact strength. The tape is 6" wide and supplied in cut-to-length rolls with an easy release paper backer. Always use RoofWrap Tape Primer to prepare the roofing membrane or application surface in advance of installing this product or any Roofwrap tape-backed product.
