Retrofit Pipe Boot

RoofWrap Retrofit Pipe Boot is two part, nylon resin curb that can be placed around pipes, conduit or wires that cannot easily be disconnected. The curb is installed to the membrane with Roofwrap Sealant and filled with Roofwrap Pourable Sealer.

Basic Uses

The Retrofit Pipe Boot is used with the RoofWrap System to seal around weatherhead roof-mounted power poles or other similar pipes measuring 1" to 3" outside diameter.

Specifications

The Retrofit Boot comes in two parts that, once installed, fit together for placement around conduit. The curb has a serviceable temperature range of -60 F to +170 F and is resistant to ozone and ultraviolet exposure.
