Termination bar

RoofWrap Termination Bar is a specially extruded aluminum bar.

Basic Uses

RoofWrap Termination Bar is designed for use as an anchor bar for attachment and termination of RoofWrap EPDM membranes and flashings. Termination Bar common use is on the perimeter the edge wall for attachment. It can also be used for field roof attachment, on support curbs, roof deck angle changes and parapet walls as an anchor or termination bar. Refer to the current RoofWrap Installation Guide for complete instructions on use.

Specifications

Material:Extruded Aluminum
Dimensions:.050" thick x 1" wide and 10" long per piece
Fastener Holes:1/4"x3/8" slotted holes on 6" centers
