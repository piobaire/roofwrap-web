Fan Vent Flashing Hood

RoofWrap Fan Vent Flashing Hoods are galvanized steel roof caps with side flashing flanges. They are available in a 5" and 7" diameter openings.

Basic Uses

RoofWrap Fan Hoods are used for weatherizing the kitchen exhaust roof conduit (5" model) and attic vents or bath exhaust roof conduit (7" model). Used with Caulk Tape, cleaner and Drill Point Screws.

Specifications

RoofWrap Fan Hoods are manufactured from galvanized steel. The 5" model flange measures 10" square, is 4" tall from the roof level and has an average 6" long connection extension below the flange level. The total free vent area is 15 square inches. The 7" model measures 12 1/4" square at the flange, 10" tall from roof level and has a 3/4" long connection extension below the flange level. The total free vent area is 30 square inches.
